The idea behind showing these models is to give the player a quick view of the actual vegetation model that corresponds with a file name. Most of the files have meaningful names however the builder will still find that he/she has to place the model in the level to see what it looks like. These will allow the builder to quickly view model images and thus save them time in level building.
