Conversation Animation List

From Dragon Age Toolset Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Each line in an animation contains animations that the actors will play while the lines are being carried out. Since the names of the animations don't give the best description of what it is and there is a reasonable delay between assigning an animation and seeing it in a preview. This is a list of some of the animation with some better descriptions.

Animation List
Name Approx Duration Description
2a misc 1 1.0 Secs Minor gesture of moving arms in front of lower waist and back again
2a misc 2 0.75 Secs Minor gesture of jerking hands slightly
2arm_beseech 5.2 Secs Adding emphasis to talking with major gestures; curcular movements and flicking towards listener; arguably not really beseeching
2arm_clench_fists 2.0 Secs Clenching both fists in front of face; blending with a weight of about 50% makes it stomach height an look more like it is an angry gesture, at 100% it just looks like a normal major gesture
2arm_emphasis2 2.25 Secs Tapping one hand in facing up palm of other hand at chest height, then doing a right-handed flick towards listener quickly before resuming pose
2arm_gesture_1 6.0 Secs Medium hand gestures in front of stomach as if explaining something
2arm_stop 2.0 Secs Hold hands in front with palms out and fingers splayed then return to sides
head gesture left 0.75 Secs Minor head jerk to the top left (their left), end result is like a nod
head gesture right 0.75 Secs Minor head jerk to the top right (their right), end result is like a nod
head_glance_left 1.6 Secs Look 90 degrees to the left for a shorter time than "headlook left"
head_glance_right 1.6 Secs Look 90 degrees to the right for a shorter time than "headlook right"
headlook left 4.8 Secs Look 90 degrees to the left, idles from 1.0 secs to 3.75 secs
headlook right 4.8 Secs Look 90 degrees to the right, idles from 1.0 secs to 3.75 secs
right hand gesture across 1.5 Secs Briefly move hand with palm upwards in front of chest
right hand gesture self 1.5 Secs Briefly move hand to stomach with palm inwards.
