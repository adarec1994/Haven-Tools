Potential conflict between add-ins can be reduced by using a unique prefix for resource names (and a similar suffix for M2DA tables). See also 2DA ranges in use.

We don't know for sure which prefixes Bioware may need to use in future, but we can reduce conflict between community offerings by registering our intentions here.

Please insert new entries in alphabetical order.

WARNING - no doubt Bioware has already allocated additional prefixes for DLC and future releases, so registering here does not guarantee that your prefix is unique.
