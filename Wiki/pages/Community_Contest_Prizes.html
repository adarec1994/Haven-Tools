Points Mean Prizes!

Typically, for any given contest, the system is likely to be:

  • 1st place: 15 points
  • 2nd place: 10 points
  • 3rd place: 5 points

Please see the FAQ below for more information on how the process works.

Redeeming Points

To exchange your points for a prize, please PM Sunjammer on Bioware Social Network.

The Prizes

The prizes we have available are subject to change and we hope to continually introduce new prizes. Work offered by fellow community members may become unavailable if that individual becomes busy, I'm sure you can understand we cant always provide our time limitlessly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if two people want and can afford the same prize but theres limited availability?

  • We generally give out a prize as soon as someone asks for it. If there is limited availability on a prize you're interested in, then PM us expressing your interest. We will reserve a prize until the next contest ends, at which point whoever reserved the prize may get it provided they have enough points. If two people reserve a prize ahead of contest results, then whoever placed highest in that contest will get the prize - the other person must choose another prize.

May I save points for use later?

  • Absolutely. However, remember that prize availability can fluctuate.

I have too few points to get a prize

  • Be sure to enter future contests to gather more points and save them up to cash them in for prizes later!
