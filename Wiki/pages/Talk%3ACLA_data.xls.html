This would appear to already be covered by CLA_base.xls. It raises a question I've left somewhat undecided in my mind, though; what's the best organization for these 2DA articles? So far I've used the filenames as page titles, but the 2DAs themselves are actually the worksheets within and individual 2DAs can span multiple M2DAs in different files to boot.

Personally, I expect that leaving it organized by filename makes the most sense. But I haven't done much 2DA work myself so ground truth may differ. Any ideas or suggestions? BryanDerksen 17:47, 21 August 2009 (UTC)
