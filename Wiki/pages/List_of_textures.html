This is a list of all the textures in vanilla Dragon Age: Origins and its DLC, including user interface textures. Duplicates have been removed from the standard-quality lists in favour of the high-quality versions; DLC have otherwise been merged. Texture atlases and speedtree textures found in the area layout .gpu.rim files have been omitted. Toolset textures have been omitted.
